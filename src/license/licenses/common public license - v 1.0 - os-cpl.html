Watson Media and IBM Cloud

IBM hosts the Masters

Everyone watching the Masters Tournament in person will walk the immaculate grounds at Augusta National. Everyone watching online will head to the digital platforms hosted on the IBM Cloud, where users can get a personalized Masters experience and AI-curated highlights.

Watson Media ingests Tournament live streams, watching for player celebration and listening for crowd and commentator excitement to identify highlight moments. Fans can easily track their favorite players and catch up on all the action with a spoiler-free mode that won’t ruin the suspense.
